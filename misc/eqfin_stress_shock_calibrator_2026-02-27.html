Method and assumptions
  1. SPX constituent weights are proxied with iShares IVV holdings, and NDX with iShares CNDX holdings, both as-of 2026-02-27.
  2. Adjusted close values are attached per ticker for 2026-02-27 via the local generator script (yfinance/Yahoo adjusted close field).
  3. Shock inputs/outputs use percent points (26 means 26%, not 0.26).
  4. Fixed-name rows use the fixed single-name shock (50% default); all other names receive the solved residual shock to hit target SPX/NDX levels.
  5. Monthly/quarterly recalibration is recommended because index weights drift and rebalances occur.
  6. Required residual shocks are solved independently for SPX and NDX from index weights and your fixed ticker list.
  7. Constituent tables show `Prior Shift %` (current anchor shocks with fixed-name overrides) and `New Shift %` (calibrated mix).
