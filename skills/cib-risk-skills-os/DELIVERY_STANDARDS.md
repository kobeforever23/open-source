# Delivery Standards
**AS-OF:** 2026-03-05 13:56:42 EST

## Deliverable classes
- Flash update (<= 1 page)
- Decision memo (1-3 pages)
- Committee pack (5-15 pages)
- Deep dive dossier (>15 pages)

## Minimum quality bar
- Accurate numbers with traceable source lineage
- Explicit assumptions and caveats
- Action owners and due dates
- Escalation logic and fallback actions
