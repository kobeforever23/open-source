# Role Paths: Analyst to ED
**AS-OF:** 2026-03-05 13:56:42 EST

## Analyst
- Focus: foundations, data controls, core risk measurement.
- Evidence: reproducible analysis + control discipline.

## Associate / AVP
- Focus: cross-risk synthesis, scenario ownership, issue closure.
- Evidence: independent decision-quality output.

## VP / ED
- Focus: enterprise trade-offs, governance leadership, talent development.
- Evidence: sustained decision impact and control maturity.
