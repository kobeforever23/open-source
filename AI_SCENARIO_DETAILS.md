## Design Summary

The scenario is built as **Severe Rates Rally plus an AI concentration overlay**.

Unchanged from Severe Rates Rally:

- Rates
- Agency
- Muni
- FX
- Broad non-AI macro backbone

Differentiated for AI severe:

- AI single names at `-50%`
- SPX headline at `-26%`
- NDX / QQQ headline at `-35%`
- QQQ / SPX April 2025 stressed vol surfaces
- AI-specific credit overlays
- Constituent-weighting control to avoid index / single-name double counting
